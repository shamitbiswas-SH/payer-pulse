CMS proposes alternatives to modify the limit on the total payment rate and other requirements for state directed payments in Medicaid managed care, citing its authority over prepaid inpatient and ambulatory health plans.

→What to do: Reshapes Medicaid managed-care revenue models and configuration. Comment closes Jul 21, 2026.

Interim Final · w/ commentMedicaidHigh

Medicaid Community Engagement (Work) Requirement

An IFC implements the new Medicaid work requirement; states must implement no later than January 1, 2027.

Read full analysisShow less

This interim final rule implements the statutory community-engagement requirement, defining which applicants and beneficiaries must demonstrate qualifying activity as a condition of eligibility. States must comply no later than January 1, 2027.

→What to do: Stand up eligibility-determination and verification workflows — a major systems-integration opportunity, with significant member-communication burden.

Vacatur of Parts of the 2024 Section 1557 Nondiscrimination Rule

A federal court vacated portions of the 2024 nondiscrimination rule (Tennessee v. Kennedy, S.D. Miss.).

Read full analysisShow less

Following an October 2025 order in Tennessee v. Kennedy (S.D. Miss.), HHS notes that portions of the 2024 Nondiscrimination in Health Programs and Activities rule — specifically those expanding the definition of sex discrimination — have been vacated.

→What to do: Review benefit-design and member-communication policies built to the 2024 standard across all affected plan lines.
